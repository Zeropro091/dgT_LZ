## PART II: BUSINESS OPERATIONS & WORKFLOW
DGT_LZ does not act like a standard "web agency". It operates like an elite tactical tech unit. The operation is divided into 4 rigid phases:

### Phase 1: The Infiltration (Lead & Audit)
- **The Hook:** We offer a 100% Free Initial Consultation (Tanpa Biaya). 
- **The Execution:** We do not pitch "AI". We ask the client: *"What repetitive task is bleeding your company's money and time?"*
- **The Deliverable:** The **AI Blueprint**. A mapped-out strategy showing exactly how replacing manual nodes with AI agents will save them thousands of hours. 

### Phase 2: The MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
- **Timeline:** 4 - 8 Weeks.
- **The Execution:** Instead of building a massive software suite at once, DGT_LZ isolates the single largest bottleneck (e.g., Customer Service, 3D printing pipeline, or supply chain data entry) and deploys an AI micro-agent to fix it immediately.
- **The Deliverable:** Proof of Concept. The client immediately sees ROI and hours saved.

### Phase 3: Autonomous Scaling (Full Integration)
- **The Execution:** Once the MVP is proven, DGT_LZ builds the full ecosystem. Legacy APIs are modernized, multiple LLM agents are connected, and precision 3D modeling/digital fabrication ecosystems are brought online if hardware is involved.
- **Result:** The company achieves true 24/7 Otonomi Sistem (Autonomous System).

### Phase 4: Ecosystem Management (Maintenance)
- **The Execution:** DGT_LZ transitions to a retainer model. We manage the cloud servers, manage the AI pipeline tokens, and ensure cybersecurity. We become their external Chief Technology Officer (CTO).

## PART IV: DESIGN STYLE & ARCHITECTURAL DOCTRINE
The visual and technical aesthetic of DGT_LZ is strictly governed by the **"Locomotive Agency" Paradigm**.

### 1. The Neo-Brutalist Aesthetic
- **Code as Architecture:** The UI is stripped of unnecessary "soft" aesthetics (like rounded pastel blobs or gradients). The design relies on exposed grids, rigid high-contrast borders (`#2A2A2A`), and dark mode mastery.
- **Color Discipline:** 
  - **Obsidian (`#0A0A0A` / `#111111`):** The void. Represents deep tech.
  - **Pristine White (`#FAFAFA`):** The structural lines and typography.
  - **Terracotta Rust (`#D46B4A`):** The "AI Spark". It is the only color allowed to pull attention. It represents raw energy and our Bali origins.

### 2. High-End Typography
Typography is treated as a structural element, not just readable text.
- **Syne (Extrabold):** Used exclusively for massive, aggressive branding and the primary logo mark. It is extended, unapologetic, and dominates the screen.
- **Clash Display:** The primary structural heading font (`h1`, `h2`). It has razor-sharp edges resembling precision-machined metal.
- **Cabinet Grotesk:** The body text. Exceptionally legible, geometric, and functional.
- **Space Mono:** Used for labels, meta-data, and technical readouts to remind the user they are interacting with an engineering firm.

### 3. The Locomotive Kinetics (Animation Philosophy)
- **Never Use Native Scroll:** DGT_LZ employs a custom physics-based scroll engine (Lenis). The scroll feels heavy, buttery, and calculated. It enforces a cinematic pacing.
- **Timeline-Based Reveals:** Animations are handled explicitly by **GSAP (GreenSock)**. Elements do not simply "fade in"; they fall into place sequentially down a timeline (`stagger`) at a locked 60 Frames Per Second.
- **Parallax as Depth:** Backgrounds and imagery move at mathematically disjointed speeds (`yPercent`) from the foreground, creating a massive sense of scale and depth without cluttering the UI.

## PART V: THE 5 AGENCY DOCTRINES
These are the unwavering rules of engagement for any DGT_LZ employee, developer, or AI agent:

1. **Standar Global, Eksekusi Presisi:** (Global Standards, Precision Execution). We are built in Bali, but our code, designs, and systems must rival Top-Tier agencies in New York or London.
2. **Mesin ROI (ROI Engine):** If a feature doesn't save the client time or make them money, we do not build it.
3. **Solusi Kustom, Bukan Templat:** (Custom Over Templates). We do not install generic WordPress themes. We build bespoke software architectures mapped directly to bottlenecks.
4. **Otonomi Tanpa Batas:** (Limitless Autonomy). The systems we build must be able to scale 10x without breaking or requiring more human hires.
5. **Estetika Adalah Fungsionalitas:** (Aesthetics ARE Functionality). A poorly designed interface destroys trust. Engineering-grade functionality must be wrapped in elite, high-end design.
